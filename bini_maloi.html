Maloi

Mary Loi Yves Kipte Ricalde

Better known as Maloi, is a Filipino rookie performer. She is the third eldest member and main vocal of the Filipino girl group BINI.

Background

Maloi's birth name is Mary Loi Yves Ricalde. Her family was originally from Lemery, Batangas where she was born. Her father is an Overseas Filipino Worker abroad, while her mother and her three siblings currently live in General Trias, Cavite.

  • Maloi's favorite color is Black
  • Maloi’s hobbies include watching K-dramas and playing guitar & ukelele.
  • Her special talent is composing songs.
  • Maloi’s favourite SHA subject is singing.
  • Her favorite Motto is:” Even if you are far away, triplihon pa an Maqueda Bay."
  • Popular line: "Lalaki po si Chowlong, 'di po siya buntis."
