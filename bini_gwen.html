Gwen

Gweneth Apuli

Better known as Gwen, is a Filipino performer and singer. She is the fourth eldest member, lead vocal & lead rapper of the Filipino girl group BINI.

Background

Gwen's birth name is Gweneth Apuli. Her Father died when she was four years old, and her mother and her 4 brothers currently live in Daraga, Albay, where she was also born and raised.

  • Gwen's favorite color is Black & Pink
  • Gwen’s hobbies are collecting heels and doing make-up.
  • Her special talent is modeling.
  • Gwen’s favorite SHA subject is singing.
  • Her favorite Motto is:” Life is not about waiting for the storm to pass but learning to dance in the rain."
  • Popular line: "Ilang banana 'yan?!"
