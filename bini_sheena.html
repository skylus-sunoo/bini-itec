Sheena

Sheena Mae Catacutan

Better known as Sheena, is a Filipino rookie performer. She is the youngest member and main dancer of the Filipino girl group BINI.

Background

Sheena's birth name is Sheena Mae Catacutan. She grew up with her single Mother and her Elder Brother. Unfortunately, her mother passed away in 2020 during her trainee days. Her grandparents and her brother currently live in Santiago City, Isabela, where she was born and raised.

  • Sheena's favorite colors are Black & Purple.
  • Sheena’s hobbies are dancing, singing, browsing social media and eating.
  • Her special talent is the ability to bend her thumb.
  • Sheena’s favorite SHA subject is dancing.
  • Her Favorite Motto is:” Never give up. Don't ever think of giving up.”
  • Popular line: "E'di 'wag mong itaga!"
  • She loves sweets and bonchon.
